About Us

Your dedicated, reliable Global Education & Visa Advisory Service in Nigeria.

Welcome To QBEE CONSULTS

QBEE CONSULTS comprises of a team of Outstanding Education Specialists who have been in the International Education Industry for over a decade and are totally committed to providing the best of services as regards placement of students across the globe and travel advisory services. We understand the unique, diverse needs of international students and we provide personalised professional services tailored to meet the needs of each client. Our fundamental goal is to provide our clients with Academic guidance, Visa advisory support and professional support as they accomplish Career and travel goals. Our team of qualified, professional people go all-out to facilitate our clients with proper guidance and ensure they secure access to Global quality Education Worldwide.
Secure your future, secure the World…
